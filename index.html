Rep. Carol Miller
Miller is the representative for West Virginia’s 3rd congressional district. She has served since Jan 3, 2019. Miller is next up for reelection in 2020.
Republican
Current Committees:
  • House Committee on Oversight and Reform
  • House Select Committee on the Climate Crisis
  • House Committee on Transportation and Infrastucture
Recent Bills enacted:

  • H.R. 4571: Accelerating Individuals into the Workforce Act
  • H.R. 3362: Small Airport Mothers’ Rooms Act of 2019
  • H.R. 3159: Reinforcing Impaired Driving Education Act of 2019
  • H.Con.Res. 10: Authorizing the use of the rotunda of the Capitol to honor the last surviving Medal of Honor recipient of the Second World War upon death.
